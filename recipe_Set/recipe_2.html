Pizza Recipe




Pizza Dough

Preparations

  • Yeast:

    I use Platinum Yeast from Red Star. I have the best results when I use this instant yeast.

    The Platinum yeast is fantastic because its careful formula strengthens your dough and makes making working with yeast simple.

    You only need 1 standard packet of yeast (2 and 1/4 teaspoons) to get the job done.

  • Water:

    I tested this pizza dough recipe with different amounts of water. 1 and 1/3 cups is the perfect amount.

    Use warm water to cut down on rise time, about 100-110°F. Anything over 130ºF kills the yeast

  • Oil:

    A couple Tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil adds wonderful flavor to the dough.

    Don’t forget to brush the dough with olive oil before adding the toppings, which prevents the crust from tasting soggy.

  • Salt:

    Salt adds necessary flavor.

  • Sugar:

    1 Tablespoon of sugar increases the yeast’s activity and tenderizes the dough, especially when paired with a little olive oil.

  • Cornmeal:

    Cornmeal isn’t in the dough, but it’s used to dust the pizza pan. Cornmeal gives the pizza crust a little extra flavor and crisp.

    Most delivery pizzas you enjoy have cornmeal on the bottom crust!


Cooking process

  1. Make the dough:

    Mix the dough ingredients together by hand or use a hand-held or stand mixer.

  2. Knead:

    Knead by hand or with your mixer. I like doing this by hand.

  3. Rise:

    Place dough into a greased mixing bowl, cover tightly, and set aside to rise for about 90 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.


Pizza assembling

Preparations

  • Make sure to read the Pizza Dough section for a better and fresh base
  • Gather your favorite toppings for the pizza assembling, These can be Pepporni, olives, Capsicum, Chicken, Cottage cheese, mozzeralla cheese
  • Make/purchase your favorite Pizza sauce and make sure it is fresh
  • Pre-heat the oven for 15 minutes

Cooking & Assembling

  1. Punch & shape:

    Punch down risen dough to release air bubbles. Divide in 2. Roll dough out into a 12-inch circle.

    Cover and rest as you prep the pizza toppings.

  2. Top it:

    Top with favorite Pizza toppings and the pizza sauce along with sprinkling cheese at top in a generous amount

  3. Bake:

    Bake pizza at a very high temperature for only about 15 minutes.
